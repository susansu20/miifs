Service 01

Kitchen Design & Consultancy

Most suppliers open with a product list. We open with your operation. Before a single piece of equipment is quoted, we study how your kitchen runs, or how a new one should run, mapping your menu, volumes, peak periods and the way your team moves, then design a layout and equipment plan built to remove bottlenecks and lift throughput.

The result is a kitchen right-sized for your business: no over-spend on capacity you will not use, no under-spec that slows you down on a busy night.

  • Workflow & efficiency assessment to find where time is lost
  • Layout & equipment planning that fits your space and budget
  • Right-sized recommendations from our 17 brands
  • Measurable targets for throughput, prep time and waste
Explore Kitchen Design & Consultancy
Service 02

Project Management

Once the plan is agreed, one team makes it real. We coordinate sourcing, customisation, delivery, installation and commissioning end to end, so you are not chasing a dozen vendors or refereeing finger-pointing on opening day.

Equipment turns up configured, fitted and working, not just delivered to a loading bay. Your project has a single owner from purchase order to first service.

  • Sourcing across 17 brands under one contact
  • Customisation handled before install
  • Delivery, install & commissioning on your timeline
  • Staff training & handover so you open ready
Explore Project Management
Service 03

Super Support

Equipment only makes money when it runs. Super Support is the after-sales service our clients talk about most, the team that answers quickly and turns up when it matters, even on weekends.

It is a retainer relationship, not a one-off invoice. Scheduled servicing catches problems before they cost you a service period, and genuine parts keep repairs lasting and warranties intact.

  • Scheduled preventive servicing to protect uptime
  • Genuine OEM parts and trained technicians
  • Fast, above-and-beyond response when you need it
  • Warranty management handled for you
Explore Super Support
Service 04

Equipment Customisation

A machine out of the box is a starting point. A large part of our value is adapting and programming equipment around your exact recipes, volumes and space, so output stays consistent no matter who is on shift.

Where one machine is not enough, we bring trusted partner suppliers in to complete the solution. The payoff is consistency and right-sized spend, with room to scale as the menu grows.

  • Menu-based configuration matched to what you serve
  • Performance tuning for your real throughput
  • Space & power adaptation so it fits safely
  • Partner-supplier integration for complete solutions
