Creatr. café: a faster beverage line that kept quality high

How a busy specialty café cut bottlenecks at the bar without adding headcount.

Client

Creatr. café

Segment

Café & Juice Bar

Location

Singapore

Service

Consult + Supply + Support

The challenge

A growing queue the bar couldn’t keep up with

At peak hours, the beverage station had become the bottleneck. Blended drinks were slowing service, texture varied between staff, and the team was reaching for more hands rather than a better setup.

The owner wanted to lift throughput and keep every cup consistent, without expanding the footprint or the headcount.

Image
What we did

Planned the line, then equipped it

A short consultation, the right machines, and configuration tuned to their menu.

Mapped the bottleneck

We watched a real peak service and pinpointed where time was being lost at the bar.

Right-sized the equipment

A commercial Vitamix line matched to their volumes, built for back-to-back blending.

Tuned to their menu

Programmed settings so every staff member produces the same texture, every time.
