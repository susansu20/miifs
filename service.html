Kitchen Design & Consultancy

We plan the kitchen before we quote the equipment.

Most suppliers start with a product list. We start with your operation, mapping how your kitchen runs, where the bottlenecks are, and the layout and equipment that will lift throughput and cut waste.

Book a consultation All services
What it covers

A plan built around your operation

Every engagement starts with your menu, volumes and space, then turns that into a working plan you can build on.

Workflow assessment

We map your menu, volumes, peak periods and staff movement to find where time and capacity are lost.

Layout planning

An equipment layout that shortens steps, smooths flow and fits your space and budget.

Equipment recommendation

The right machines from our 17 brands, sized and specced to your real-world demand, not a generic spec.

Customisation advice

How each machine should be configured and tuned to your menu before it is installed.

Measurable targets

Clear goals for throughput, prep time and waste, so success is something you can actually measure.

One accountable partner

The same team carries the plan through sourcing, installation and ongoing support.
