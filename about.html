About Us · Est. 2015

A boutique foodservice partner that dares to innovate.

We don’t just sell equipment. We help F&B businesses run better.

Mii FoodService is a boutique food services and equipment company, dedicated to simple, effective custom solutions backed by proven foodservice concepts. Since 2015, we’ve grown from an equipment distributor into a kitchen solutions partner across Singapore and Malaysia.

Book a consultation
Our story

From supplying machines to shaping kitchens

We started by distributing some of the world’s best commercial kitchen brands, Vitamix, Zumex, BUNN, Hallde and more. But our clients kept coming back for something money can’t buy off a shelf: advice, customisation, and after-sales service that actually shows up.

Over the years that became our real business. Today we lead with the solution, helping operators plan efficient kitchens, then supplying and maintaining the equipment that makes them work. Equipment sales come after the right advice, not before it.
